The Twitter Files

An archive of tweet threads sent by Matt Taibbi (@mtaibbi) and Bari Weiss (@bariweiss). Each file was created by pasting "Embed Tweet" text from Twitter, and depends on the continued existence of the tweets on Twitter for all but the text. At the top of each file is a link to am MHTML file, containing the whole thing with NO reliance on Twitter.

  1. The Twitter Files by Matt Taibbi, December 2, 2022
  2. Twitter Files Supplemental by Matt Taibbi, December 6, 2022
  3. The Twitter Files Part Two by Bari Weiss, December 8, 2022
  4. The Twitter Files - The Removal of Donald Trump, Part One by Matt Taibbi, December 9, 2022
